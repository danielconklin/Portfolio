What I do

Songwriting

I take students through the entire songwriting process, from creating chord progressions and melodes to building out song structure. Using a number of writing exercises and techniques, I help students build out tlyrics for verses, choruses, bridges, and more. We also work on demoing out songs to hear back what they sound like.

Music Production

Production takes the creative process a step forward, by utilizing a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) such as Logic Pro X, Pro Tools, or Studio One to record, edit, and mix musical compositions. Students learn how to create interesting and unique arrangements and use plugins to sculpt the sounds of the songs they work on.

Instrument Lessons

I teach a number of instruments for students of all ages. Lessons include beginner and intermediate piano and voice, as well as advanced guitar and bass. Students learn warmup exercises, proper technique, and how to read both tablature and standard notation.

Who I am

Songwriter and Producer based out of Seattle, WA

I am a music instructor with over 15 years of experience teaching aspiring musicians of all ages and skill levels. Having been a teacher and Music Director both privately and at a nubmer of music schools, I take a hands-on approach in guiding my students to challenge themselves both technically and creatively.

As a songwriter and performer, my work has been featured on Cartoon Network, Netflix, The Disney Channel, and more. I've worked with students and artists who've had placements in film and television, and continue to work as as producer for a number of aspiring artists and musicians.

Guitar and Songwriting can be taken as group sessions for classes upto 4 students. Classes are offered as a minimum of 2 90-minute sessions or 3 60-minute sessions
